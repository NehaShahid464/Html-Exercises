Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my Favourire Foods. Here is the recipie for Deliciously Scrambled Eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1tbs Butter
  • 2tbs Cream

Method

  1. Melt Butter in a frying pan over a medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the Eggs and Cream in a bowl.
  3. Once the butter has melted add eggs and cream now.
  4. Using a spatula folt the Eggs from the edge of the pan to the centre every 20 seconds as if you are making an omelete.
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the Heat.
